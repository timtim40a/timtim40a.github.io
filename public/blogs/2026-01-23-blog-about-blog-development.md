#### Here we are

It has always been difficult for me to write blogs or lead any kind of social life on the internet. However, earlier this year I realised that the problem was not my lack of literacy, charisma or extraversy, but the services I used to post. Everyhing, from Instagram to LinkedIn started to become bland and not customisable at all. That is why I decide to post something on here time to time. In this space I can do whatever I want to my texts , visually and sensually.

I used to write journals, but every single one of them was inconclusively abandoned half or even quarter of the way there. Arrogantly, I want to say that so many brilliant ideas I would like to share with the world are stuck in some brand-new notebook somewhere on the top shelf. I don't know if this blog is what will rescue my thoughts and learnings from the inevitable abyss of forgetfulness and laziness. But it is surely a step.

I like the fact that I don't know who is reading this, it doesn't matter much. But I am glad someone is reading this!

#### What will be

I will post about my web dev, programming, and gaming endeavours. Sometimes with a sprinkle of my _highly educated artistic_ view. Or maybe even drafts of something that may become a scientific paper in the future. If someone wants to follow this, drop me a mail on timtim40a@gmail.com and I will definitely create some kind of newsletter. Not sure it will ever happen, however you are free to prove me wrong!
